Airpho WiFi

Connect to the WiFi network of a Airpho device.

Method:

1.Tap Settings,then W-iFi.

2.Select the SSID then go to WiFi. And connect to the WiFi network. (SSID are specified on the label of the device).

3.Return to APP once connected to mesh network.

Why does nothing happen after connection?

Why does nothing happen after connection?

1.Please ensure that it is connected to the WiFi network of Airpho mesh.


2.Only series of mesh are supported, such as M400, M405, M400P.


3.If you want to control a mesh, verify that it is not controlled by others.
